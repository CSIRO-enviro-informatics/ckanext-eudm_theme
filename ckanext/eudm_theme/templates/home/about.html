About EUDM

The Energy Use Data Model (EUDM) is a platform for discovering data, research and reports from across the energy sector. Our focus is on providing you with a single access point to the best resources for understanding what drives and shapes Australian energy consumption behaviour, from individual households and businesses to our national energy use trends. The team behind EUDM curates public data assets, works with data holders to release previously private data, deploys cutting-edge data science techniques to expand the value of existing data, and leads brand new data collection and research efforts to fill critical gaps for the energy sector. We collaborate with industry, science and government organisations, and engage with the Australian people to deliver a diverse collection of assets that will inform future Australian decision making across the sector.

EUDM is currently in a pilot phase and what you see at the moment is just our first steps into improving data availability and delivering high quality data to the Australian community. Across the next few years the resources provided through EUDM will grow dramatically and the platform itself will continue to evolve to fit the needs of our users.
